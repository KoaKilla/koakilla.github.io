About Me

Front End Developer

Hello! I am a 25 year old, learning developer with a deep hunger for knowledge and new experiences. I got into programming a couple years ago when some close friends of mind started to get into the industry and started showing me the complexity of the internet and how much work and effort is put into putting it all together. It seemed like a fun challenge for myself as I was always into tech and it seemed like an industry that would fit me perfectly! I began learning the basics and became very interested in the css and javascipt aspect of coding. It provided me with the a creative outlet and got me hooked instantly. There was of course huge challenges to overcome with learning to program but my expeirence in the media production industry drove my desire to learn more about the creative aspects of code. I hope this webpage provides a good look into what I enjoy creating and also opens new doors for me to explore and learn!
